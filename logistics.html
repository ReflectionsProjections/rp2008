Parking
For Friday, parking tags can be purchased from the University: Parking Tag Information. On Saturday and Sunday, there are several parking lots near the Siebel Center that have free parking.

The campus parking office is, conveniently, less than a block from the Siebel Center. Please see this map to locate the office and the neighboring parking lots. Siebel Center is building 563 on the map.
